Sprint Routine

This routine is highly recommended for building raw, explosive power for BMX gate starts and straightaway speed:

  • Warm Up (5 mins): Stay in a light gear, spin at a high cadence (90+ RPM) to get blood flowing.
  • 10 Sprints (10 mins): At the start of every minute, sprint max out-of-the-saddle for 10 seconds. Spin easy for the remaining 50 seconds.
  • Voldoen / Cooldown (5 mins): Spin out the lactic acid. Easy, smooth pedaling back to baseline.
